My Letter To You 💌

Dear Babu,

Happy Birthday, my love. ❤️

I know birthdays are usually filled with hugs, gifts, cakes, and being surrounded by the people you love. If I could choose one thing today, it would simply be to be there with you, celebrating beside you. But until that day comes, I wanted to create something that could carry a small piece of my heart to you.

Sometimes I think about how strange it is that someone can become such an important part of your life. Out of all the people in this world, somehow our paths crossed, and my days have never felt the same since. You've become a part of my routine, my thoughts, my happiest moments, and even the things I look forward to when life feels difficult.

Thank you for being patient with me, for listening to my endless stories, for making me laugh when I need it the most, and for caring about me in ways that words can never fully explain. You have this beautiful ability to make ordinary days feel special without even trying.

Even though distance keeps us apart, you've never felt far from my heart. Every call, every message, every little moment we share means more to me than you probably realize. There are days when I miss you a little extra, but even then, thinking about you somehow makes me smile.

Today is your day, and more than anything, I want you to remember how loved you are. Not just by your family and friends, but by me. Deeply, genuinely, and endlessly.

I hope this year brings you happiness, success, good health, and countless reasons to smile. I hope your dreams come true, your worries become lighter, and that life is always kind to you.

And I hope that whenever you come back to this little website, whether it's tomorrow, next month, or years from now, it reminds you of one simple thing:

You are one of the best things that has ever happened to me.

P.S.
No matter how many birthdays pass, I'll always be grateful that I get to celebrate yours. ❤️

Happy Birthday, Babu. ❤️

I love you💋.

Forever yours,
— shlok
